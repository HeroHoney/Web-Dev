Seafood Pasta

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
olive oil 2 tbsp
shrimp / prawns peeled and deveined 250g
mussel meat 150g
calamari tubes / squid heads 150g
butter 2 tbsp
garlic cloves crushed 4
chilli flakes ½ tsp
white wine ½ cup
lemon juice 1-2 tsp
cream 1 cup
salt and pepper to taste
parsley finely chopped ½ cup
pasta 500 g

Step 1: In a large pan, add a splash of oil and allow to heat up.

Step 2: Season the seafood with salt and pepper then cook in batches until golden brown.

Step 3: The shrimp/prawns should take no more than a minute per side (depending on size) and the calamari and mussels will take only a few minutes to cook. Take care not to overcook the seafood as they will go tough and rubbery. Rather undercook slightly as it will cook a little more while being tossed with the sauce and pasta.

Step 4: Remove the seafood from the pan and set aside.

Step 5: In the same pan, melt the butter and add the garlic and chilli flakes. Cook until fragrant then pour in the lemon juice and wine.

Step 6: In the same pan, melt the butter and add the garlic and chilli flakes. Cook until fragrant then pour in the lemon juice and wine.

Step 7:In the same pan, melt the butter and add the garlic and chilli flakes. Cook until fragrant then pour in the lemon juice and wine.

Step 8: Add the seafood (and any of its resting juices) and parsley and stir to combine.

Step 9: Toss the sauce with cooked pasta and a splash of the pasta cooking water and toss to coat the pasta. Serve sprinkled with parsley.
